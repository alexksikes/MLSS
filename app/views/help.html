Welcome to the MLSS admin. Applications to the Machine Learning Summer School are received from this form and go into the No reference folder. The referee of this application is notified by email and asked to provide a reference using this form. After the reference is received, the application goes into To Review.

Applications for people who are affiliated with the university (no reference needed, no travel or accomodation support) may be received from a separate internal form and go straight into To Review.

The main page to the admin is password protected. Anyone who has access to the admin can browse through the records but in order to rate / admit / reject applicants, you will need to register. All your reviews could then be found on My Reviews.
